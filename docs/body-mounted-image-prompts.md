# ChatGPT Image Generation Prompts — Body-Mounted Camera (IronPal)

> **Logo reference:** All prompts use the IronPal "Iron Ring" logo — the sole selected brand mark. The logo consists of a bold electric teal (`#00E5CC`) circular ring with a thick stroke (~15% of diameter), a clean gap/break at the 6 o'clock position (bottom), and a small solid teal circle (camera lens) at the 12 o'clock position (top center) inside the ring, paired with the "IRONPAL" wordmark in all-caps, wide-tracked, bold sans-serif (Montserrat Bold style) in ice white (`#F0F4F8`). See `docs/logo-design-concepts.md` for full specification and `input/images/logo/v4/` for generated references.
