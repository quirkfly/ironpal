## 2.4 — Character/Face Consistency Plan

Per the execution plan (Section "Key Technical Considerations"), character consistency must be managed across specific shot groups.

### Consistency Groups

| Group | Shots | Character | Consistency Requirement | Strategy |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **A — "Old Way" Male** | S2a | Athletic male, dark hair, sleeveless black shirt | Low — single shot, different character from IronPal group is acceptable | No action needed |
| **B — "Old Way" Female** | S2b | Athletic female, black sports bra, ponytail | Low — single shot, different character from IronPal group is acceptable | No action needed |
| **C — IronPal Male** | S4a, S4c, S5 | Athletic male, dark brown hair, light stubble, black sleeveless shirt, IronPal headband | **HIGH** — same athlete across bench press, dumbbell curls, and payoff phone scene. This is the primary character arc. | Use Runway Gen-4 character reference to lock face. Current generations already show reasonable similarity (similar build, hair, complexion). Accept minor variation — the headband anchors identity across shots. |
| **D — IronPal Female** | S4b | Athletic female, brown ponytail, black sports bra, IronPal headband | Medium — single shot, but ideally visually distinct from S6a/S6c female | Current S4b female has slightly different features from S6a/S6c females. Acceptable. |
| **E — Social Proof Female** | S6a, S6c | Athletic female, brown ponytail, black sports bra, IronPal headband | Medium — these are quick montage cuts, but ideally the SAME person across both to read as "diverse athletes" (with S6b being a different male athlete). | Current S6a/S6c images appear to be the same face/model. Good — this works for the montage. Keep as is. |
| **F — Social Proof Male** | S6b | Athletic male, baseball cap, black tank top | Low — single shot, intentionally different from Group C to show diversity | No action needed. Cap (vs headband) already differentiates this character. |

### Consistency Assessment

- **Group C is the critical chain.** S4a, S4c, and S5 must read as the same person. Current images show a similar-looking male across all three (dark brown hair, similar build, clean-shaven/light stubble). The headband is the strongest anchor.
- **Groups E works well** — the S6a and S6c females look like the same person, which helps the quick montage read as "same athlete, different exercises."
- **No cross-group consistency is needed** — the "old way" athletes (Groups A, B) are intentionally different from the "IronPal way" athletes.

### Recommended Post-Production Consistency Fixes

1. Color-grade all "IronPal way" shots (S3-S5) with the same warm amber LUT to unify the look even if faces vary slightly.
2. Ensure the teal LED glow is composited at the same intensity/size across all headband shots (S4a, S4b, S4c, S5, S6a, S6c).
3. Overlay crisp "IronPal" text on the headband in post if the AI rendering is too blurry in any shot (per execution plan recommendation).
